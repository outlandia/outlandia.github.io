About

OUTLANDIA is a 2D role-playing adventure, set in a universe where Western North America has been burdened with the arrival of a magical substance, Magik, VIA a comet. You, the protagonist, a young and developing mage, must quickly learn the ways of Magik to defend your newfound home, Skala Village, from a ever-changing dangerous world.

With an emphasis on survival, exploration, character customization and base-building, you will have lots of options to grow your character in unique ways to keep the residents of Skala Village safe.

Alpha Release: 01.01.2022
